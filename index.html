The Energy Problem

Over 2.5 billion people worldwide lack sustainable access to electricity, forcing them to rely on expensive and polluting fossil fuels for their energy needs. Even those with access to electricity face rising costs and environmental concerns.

Current solar charging solutions lack smart optimization, making them inefficient and unreliable when people need power the most. This creates a barrier to digital inclusion and sustainable development.
